The Best Grilled Steak

For all the meat lovers out there, we can all agree that steak is up there, here is a grilled steak which was cooked on coal fire.

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye steak
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1-2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Fresh roesmary or thyme (optional)

Steps

  1. Let the steak sit at room temperature for 30 minutes
  2. Pat it dry with a paper towel, then season generously with salt
  3. Heat a cast iron skillet over high heat until very hot.
  4. Place the steak in the pan and sear for 2-3 minutes without moving it
  5. Flip the steak and add butter, garlic, and herbs to the pan
  6. Baste the steak with melted butter for another 2-3 minutes
  7. Remove the steak and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving
